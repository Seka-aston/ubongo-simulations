Our Mission

To empower African university instructors with AI-powered tools that make experiential learning accessible, affordable, and contextually relevant, built through genuine co-creation, not extraction.

Our Vision

A future where every African university student has access to world-class experiential learning, simulations that reflect their context, their challenges, and their aspirations.

WHAT WE'RE BUILDING

AI-powered simulations designed
for African university classrooms

From medical case studies to business negotiations, Ubongo Simulations gives instructors
the tools to create realistic, interactive learning scenarios.

AI Scenario Builder

Describe a learning objective. The AI generates a multi-path simulation with realistic characters, decisions, and consequences.

Student Role-Play

Students step into real-world roles, policy analyst, entrepreneur; making decisions that shape the simulation's outcome.

Learning Analytics

Track how students navigate choices, where they struggle, and what they learn, giving you clear insights into comprehension.

WHY WE NEED YOU

We're building with you,
not for you.

Most edtech products are designed in Silicon Valley and dropped into African classrooms. We're taking a different approach. We believe the best tools come from deep collaboration with the people who'll use them every day.

  • You shape the product before a single line of code is written
  • Your classroom realities guide every design decision
  • Early access to a tool built around your actual needs
